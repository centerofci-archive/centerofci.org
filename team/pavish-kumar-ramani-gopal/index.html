Mathesar

Pavish Kumar Ramani Gopal is a software engineer with a focus on building products that simplify complex usecases. At CCI, he works on Mathesar to help databases and data manipulation become more accessible for users of all skill levels. His past experiences include building SIEM solutions, log management products and E-commerce platforms. Apart from writing code, he likes to sketch, and go on treks.
